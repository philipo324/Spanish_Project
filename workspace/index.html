Welcome to Spanish Comparatives

This website was created to help tutor and help Spanish students who need help with Spanish Comparatives.

Ok, let's start off with the basic types of comparatives: Mas….que Menos….que Tan….como. This is what will be covered in this section.

Comparative Equations

Mas + adverb/adjective/noun + que (more….than)
Menos + adverb/adjective/noun +que (less than)
No + verb + tan + adjective/adverb + como (not as….as)

Examples

Corre mas rapido que ella. -He/she runs faster than her
Tengo menos libros que el. -I have less books than him
Samuel no es tan alto como su hermano. -Samuel is not as tall as his brother
